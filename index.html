Snake Game

Welcome to this mini game.

Simply put, this is the classic Snake game. You run around on the board as a snake. The goal is to eat and grow. Most importanly, avoid munching on yourself!

Game controls:

  • WSAD : control the direction of movement
  • Enter: start
  • Space: pause/unpause
  • R: restart

Good luck! 😉
