Snake Game

Welcome to this mini game.

Simply put, this is classic Snake game. You play as a snake to run around on the board, eat and grow, and most importanly, avoid taking a big munch on yourself!

Game controls:

  • WSAD : control the direction of movement
  • Space: pause/unpause
  • R: restart

Good luck! 😉
